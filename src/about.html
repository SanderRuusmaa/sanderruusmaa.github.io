A little bit about us.

Established in 2020, Desynchronised is a startup production company founded by Oliver Kiisa, looking to tell stories people can throw themselves into.

What do we do?


The company focuses on developing film ideas and bringing them to your screens. Our goal is to produce quality features, shorts, commercials and documentaries that please the viewers.


For the past three years Oliver Kiisa has worked on productions of different scales - from short films to commercials, from indie to studio projects. Having gone through different departments, it is with great certainty that Production is the field Oliver loves to work in.


Desynchronised also provides production services to others who are in need for extra hands. If there's a project in need for assistance, then our company can offer the manpower to help finish the project hassle-free.
